How to cook "Pizza"

Description

Pizza, a beloved Italian dish, features a crispy crust topped with a savory tomato sauce, gooey cheese, and various toppings of your choice. Making pizza at home allows you to customize it to your liking and enjoy it fresh out of the oven. Here's a simple recipe to create your own pizza.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Let the Dough Come to Room Temperature.
  2. Prepare Your Surface With Oil.
  3. Shape the Dough.
  4. Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
